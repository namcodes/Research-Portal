Welcome to the Research Portal

This research portal targets for utilization by individuals in the academe such as student-researchers and teacher-researchers of Tarlac City Schools Division. This portal aims to provide researchers with references, updates, and trends about education. The Research Management Guidelines of DepEd is incorporated in this portal for easy access by the teacher-researchers. Learning Materials (LMs), Curriculum Guides (CGs) and Most Essential Learning Competencies (MELCs) for Senior High School's Practical Research 1 and 2 are incorporated in this portal. Other files and downloadable forms are made available in this portal. Enjoy while exploring the world of research!
